# n8n Community Entry Plan

Repository: https://github.com/kostasuser01gr/n8n-local-automation-portfolio

## First Steps

1. Complete verified workflow execution evidence.
2. Post a short introduction.
3. Read and respond to relevant workflow-building questions.
4. Share the job-tracker case study for feedback.
5. Improve the workflow based on replies.

## Tone

- Helpful
- Specific
- Transparent about limitations
- Not promotional
- No job begging

## Topics To Contribute On

- PostgreSQL persistence
- self-hosted n8n debugging
- import/export workflows
- validation and rejection handling
- local AI workflows
- workflow documentation
