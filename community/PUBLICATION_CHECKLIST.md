## Before Posting

- Confirm the public repository is reachable.
- Confirm README screenshots render.
- Confirm no credential IDs or private paths are visible.
- Confirm sample data is synthetic.
- Confirm validation and secret scan scripts pass.
- Keep the post technical, not promotional.
- Mention limitations clearly.

## Suggested Post Order

1. Introduction post.
2. Job Tracker tutorial.
3. Fleet Operations case study.
4. Local AI Knowledge tutorial.
5. Template submission draft.

## Do Not Publish Automatically

Community posts should be reviewed manually before posting.
