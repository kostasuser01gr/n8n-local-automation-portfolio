The Fleet Operations workflow turns vehicle intake data into operational priorities and persisted vehicle state.

Verified result:

- 3 fleet vehicles persisted
- 1 operation run recorded
- 2 high-priority vehicles identified

The workflow demonstrates validation, normalization, priority classification, idempotent upsert, and audit-run persistence. It intentionally uses synthetic sample records so it can be shared without customer or employee data.
